Manage Your Chess Club with Ease

OpenChessClub is an open-source platform for managing players, tournaments, and club data, built for organizers, by players.

Tournament Management

Create, pair, and track chess tournaments with automatic standings and results.

Player Database

Maintain player profiles, rating history, and performance analytics with ease.

Club Insights

Track club growth, attendance, and player engagement with built-in reporting tools.

Open Source & Free

Completely open, self-hostable, and community-driven. Your club, your data.
